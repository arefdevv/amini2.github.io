Marriage Process

1. Contact me today to fill out a Notice of Intended Marriage (NOIM). You can also download the form here.

2. Once the Notice of Intended Marriage (NOIM) form is filled out, it must remain with me for a period of one calendar month. During this time, I will also help you fill out all the relevant documentation and meet any identity requirements.

3. On your wedding day, I will officially solemnise your marriage in the presence of the Groom and Bride as well as two witnesses.

4. I will then apply for an Official Marriage Certificate through the Marriage Registry Department.

5. After 4 – 6 weeks, your marriage will be registered with the Marriage Registry Department, and your Official Marriage Certificate will be delivered to you by mail.
